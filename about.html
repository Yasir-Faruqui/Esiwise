ABOUT ESIWISE

Helping businesses deliver and generate real measurable value

Esiwise was founded in 2018 on a disruptive idea: that eProcurement can unlock significant strategic value from a company’s spend and supply chain.

A global provider of industry-leading cloud solutions that help organizations gain complete visibility into their data; turn it into valuable insights; and achieve total control to make more intelligent, confident business decisions. Through end-to-end source-to-pay and contract management, our next-generation tools empower you to proactively eliminate risks, cut costs and save money.
