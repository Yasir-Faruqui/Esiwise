E-PROCUREMENT

Simplified purchasing for modern businesses

Procurement as an enterprise strategy

Software as a solution SAAS platform that enables an organizations to automate it’s entire purchasing processes, from catalog management to requisitions, purchase orders (PO), invoicing, suppliers and more. Also called procure-to-pay (P2P) solutions, these tools replace many if not most manual processes with workflow automation. This eliminates many manual errors, streamlines the purchasing function, helps to control spend and increase savings and mitigates the chance of maverick (off-contract) spend.

  • Modern, simple digital experience
  • Spend Insights to make intelligent decisions
  • Maintain and ensure compliance

Esiwise eProcurement Solution provides a single solution to automate your key supply chain processes with an all encompassing source to pay process.

Electronic Procurement

Access greater transparency and control of spend. Eliminate the email and paper process and replace it with a fully traceable and audit-able purchasing process. Eliminate maverick spend with budget approval workflows and contracted pricing for improved spend management.

Purchase Requisitions

Easy process for buyers to make purchase requests using integrated product catalogs with contracted pricing. Multi-tiered approval workflows to ensure compliance with budgets.

Request For Quotes

Generate greater cost savings with the use of electronic auctions. Use the RFQ process to get preliminary quotes followed by a Reverse Auction for further price reduction.

Reverse Auctions

Easily create purchase orders, receive against purchase orders and match with supplier invoices. Simple three way match ensures that accounts payable matches actual product/service received.

Purchase Order & Invoice Managemens

Manage all your supplier information in one single repository. Create supplier product catalogs along with contracted pricing for streamlining the purchasing process. Online supplier catalogs allow for creating a guided buying experience for your purchasing teams. Transact seamlessly with all your suppliers using contracted pricing for a seamless ordering process.

Supplier & Contract Management

Generate a Quote request from a Purchase Requisition for securing pricing from multiple approved suppliers. Ability to select the best supplier offer and award electronically as well as generate a purchase order.
